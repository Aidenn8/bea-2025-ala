Abstract

We present a system for Track 1 (Mistake Identification) of the BEA 2025 Shared Task, focused on evaluating AI tutors in mathematical dialogue settings. Our model, based on a fine-tuned BERT encoder, identifies pedagogical errors in tutor responses and achieved a macro-F1 score of 0.6475 on the validation set. We placed 28th globally, contributing to research in AI tutor evaluation.
